================================================================================
KEY INNOVATION: SEMANTIC PRESERVATION
================================================================================

Unlike traditional audiobooks or ebooks that are locked in specific formats,
the CodeBase project uses SPKML to preserve the story semantically.

This means:

✅ Same screenplay generates all formats (HTML, PDF, EPUB, Audio, etc.)
✅ Story remains readable and reformattable for decades
✅ No vendor lock-in (SPKML is open standard)
✅ Machine-readable for future adaptations
✅ Complete attribution and provenance preserved
✅ Easy to generate new formats as technology evolves

When AI systems improve, new formats emerge, or platforms change—
the original SPKML screenplay remains the master source.
